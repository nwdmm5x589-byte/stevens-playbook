Deed Restriction Rules That Directly Affect You (2025 CC&Rs)

Build Plan Restrictions

C.2(a) — Only ONE single-family dwelling per lot Outbuildings to serve the residents may be erected, but a MIL suite as a separate rental unit likely counts as a second dwelling. Must clarify with ACC.
C.16 — No outbuilding shall be used as a dwelling "No shack or any outbuilding (other than a private boathouse, garage, or storage building) shall be erected... and no boathouse, garage or storage building shall at any time be used as a dwelling, temporarily or permanently." A garage with a living space above may violate this.
C.5(a) — Minimum 1,200 sqft for new permanent structures Living area (excluding porches, garages, carports, servants quarters) must be at least 1,200 sqft for new structures. Does not apply to your existing 1,746 sqft house. Would apply to a new standalone MIL suite.

Setbacks & Building Limits

C.4 — Setbacks: 20ft front, 10ft rear, 5ft sides Main residence: 20ft from front property line, 10ft from rear. Detached garage/carport/other structure: 5ft from interior side lot lines. Max 2 stories, max 35ft floor-to-roof-peak.
C.5(d) — No sheet metal roof or siding without ACC approval No tar type roof/siding either. All exterior must be painted or stained. Must be underpinned/underskirted (no visible piers).
C.5(c) — All construction must use new materials No used buildings or structures moved onto lot without ACC written permission. Stone, brick OK. Antique decorative with ACC approval.
C.3(c) — Exterior must be complete within 6 months of starting If not commenced within 1 year of ACC approval, approval is void. No occupancy until connected to public sewer and water.

STR & Rental Rules

C.2(b) — Rentals/leases are allowed but with conditions Any rental or lease must provide in writing that the renter has received a copy of the Deed Restrictions and agrees to be bound by them. You must advise renters of all rules. Owner is responsible for renter violations.
C.2(b) — "Boarding houses, hotels" are prohibited The restrictions exclude "boarding houses, hotels, and all other commercial uses." STRs are a gray area — rentals are explicitly allowed, but the "hotel" language could be used against you. Shelter Cove is marketed as STR-friendly, so this likely isn't enforced, but know the risk.
C.2(e) — Max 2 unrelated persons per household "Single family" is defined as 1 person, family members, or no more than 2 unrelated persons. Airbnb groups of 6-10 unrelated guests technically violate this. Unlikely to be enforced against short-term guests, but it's in the CC&Rs.

Old Garage — Urgent

C.12 — Dilapidated structures must be fixed or removed in 60 days "If vacancies occur and said structures fall into disrepair, they will be deemed 'attractive nuisances', and the Owner will be required to dismantle and remove said structures." If the POA notices your old garage, they can force removal at your cost and lien your property.

Other Rules to Know

C.11 — Pets: max 3 of each type (dogs, cats) Dogs and cats OK. No livestock. Must comply with Polk County Animal Control. Guest dogs count — make sure your listing cap aligns.
C.5(h) — Fences: wood or steel only facing streets Allowed to side and back lot lines, and to no less than 15ft from front lot lines. No barbwire facing streets. No storing items against fences.
C.10(g) — Grass max 12 inches, maintain culverts/ditches Owner responsible for mowing easements, vegetation crossing property lines, keeping culverts and drainage ditches clear.
C.8 — No vehicles/boats parked on right-of-way or common areas No vehicles blocking public roads. Must be rid of non-running vehicles, unused appliances, scrap lumber, tires, trash. "Restored" vehicles must be covered and hidden from public view.
C.6 — You can consolidate your 2 lots into 1 building site As owner of Lots 2 & 3, you can combine them. Side setbacks measured from outer property lines. Must maintain minimum frontage.

Long-Term Guest Policy — When to Say No
The young adult situation (parents booked for 20-year-olds for 3 months, left house dirty) is a recoverable lesson. Here's how to prevent it going forward.
No monthly bookings May–October, period
Block monthly pricing in your calendar for those months. Non-negotiable.
For 30+ day stays, require a video call first
Anyone staying a month or more should speak with you. You'll know in 5 minutes if they're the right fit.
Require a damage deposit for all monthly stays
Airbnb's standard policies may not cover damage from a dirty house. A separate damage deposit agreement gives you recourse.
Midstay check-in at 30 days for multi-month
For a 2–3 month stay, do a quick walk-through or send your cleaner at the 30-day mark. Catch problems before they become disasters.
